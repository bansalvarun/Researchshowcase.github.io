What is Research SHowcase?

The IIIT Delhi Research Showcase is annual two-day event to showcase to the world, the research and development efforts of the students of IIIT Delhi. IIIT Delhi is a research lead organization and continuously efforts to carry out research and development in information and software technologies, and in leveraging IT in specific domain areas. This vision is reflected in course structure and general outlook of the students, leading towards several innovative ideas, projects and prototypes. These posters and demos include some of the recent innovations by various research groups at IIIT-D including Emerging Architecture and System Design, Image Analysis and Biometrics , Information Management and Data Analytics, Mobile and Ubiquitous computing, Security and Privacy, Software Engineering and Theory as well as outstanding course projects by students as part of their curriculum

Who are invited?

Research Showcase is intended for faculty of other institutes, industry professionals, media, students, parents and other tech-savvy visitors. Anyone interested to visit IIIT-Delhi campus to interact with students and provide feedback on their display are cordially invited. We particularly invite students and esteemed faculty members from various technical institutes all over India. Pre-registration for the event is mandatory.

Where?

RS '14 will take place in IIIT Delhi's Okhla campus.
